# Proposal and Quote Templates

Reusable templates the pricing-desk fills in. Currency aware, with NPR and USD variants. Keep the tone clear and confident. Lead with a "starts from" figure. No em dashes, ever.

## Tiered quote (good, better, best)

Always present three options. The high tier sets the frame and the middle tier should look like the sensible choice.

```
Quote for: [client name]
Project: [short description of the outcome]
Prepared: [date]
Starts from: [currency][lowest tier figure]

Tier 1, Essential ([currency][amount])
  - [deliverable]
  - [deliverable]
  - [revision count] rounds of revisions
  - Timeline: [weeks]

Tier 2, Recommended ([currency][amount])
  - Everything in Essential, plus
  - [deliverable]
  - [deliverable]
  - [revision count] rounds of revisions
  - Timeline: [weeks]

Tier 3, Complete ([currency][amount])
  - Everything in Recommended, plus
  - [deliverable]
  - [deliverable]
  - Priority turnaround
  - Timeline: [weeks]

Deposit: [50 percent for foreign clients] to begin.
Valid for: 14 days.
```

## Scope of work

```
Scope of Work
Client: [name]    Date: [date]    Currency: [NPR or USD]

What is included
  - [clear deliverable with a number where possible]
  - [clear deliverable]

What is not included
  - [the common scope creep item, named up front]

Revisions
  - [number] rounds are included. Further rounds are billed at
    [rate] per round.

Change orders
  - Any work outside the list above is a change order. We will
    quote it before starting, and it does not begin until approved.

Timeline
  - [milestone]: [date]
  - [milestone]: [date]

Payment
  - Deposit of [amount] to begin.
  - Balance due [on delivery or on milestone].
```

## Invoice

```
Invoice [number]
From: [studio or owner name]
To: [client name and address]
Date: [date]    Due: [date]    Currency: [NPR or USD]

  Description                          Amount
  [line item]                          [currency][amount]
  [line item]                          [currency][amount]
  ----------------------------------------------
  Subtotal                             [currency][amount]
  [tax or VAT if applicable]           [currency][amount]
  Total due                            [currency][amount]

Pay to: [bank or Wise or Payoneer details]
Note: [thank you line, payment terms]
```

## Notes for filling these in

- Read nepal-foreign-pricing first to pick the right card and the right anchor.
- For foreign clients use USD and a 50 percent deposit. For local clients use NPR and consider phasing the work instead of discounting.
- Confirm tax and forex handling with a local accountant. These templates are logistics, not financial or legal advice.
